Progress

This week, we drew up a rough sketch of our idea which includes the different compoents, software (Tableau, SQL, etc.), hardware (rfid reader, speakers, cameras, etc.) as well as other items we would be including in our project. This sketch combines all our ideas together on what we find plausible and finalises our project map moving forward. We also completed a list of items which we would require from our sponsors. With this, we are also trying to split up our roles to be in charge of different areas prefably roles that we are comfortable with and stronger in.

Contributions

As seen above, we have completed the NOT list which puts the stakes in the ground and sets expectations around what we are going to be doing for this project. We have also finalised our pitch as well as our project name and logo which presents our brand to our audience. Our group agreed on the name Project SmoothStart as we all agree that it sounds meaningful and aligned with our motto and goals.
